24-Hour Cancellation Rule

Most airlines allow free cancellation within 24 hours of booking if the flight is scheduled at least 7 days later.

Refund Eligibility

  • Refundable tickets: Eligible for cash refunds.
  • Non-refundable tickets: Usually eligible for credit or travel vouchers.

Flight Changes

Many airlines have removed change fees (except for basic economy fares). Refunds may depend on fare type.

Name Corrections

Minor spelling corrections are typically allowed with a fee. Full name transfers are not permitted.

Disclaimer

Billpy Services LLC is not affiliated with any airline. Refund policies vary by carrier and should be confirmed directly with the airline.
